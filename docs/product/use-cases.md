# Use Cases

## Mall Assistant

Robots help visitors navigate shopping malls, answer questions, and provide directions.

Capabilities:

- speech interaction
- navigation
- map visualization
- store information retrieval

---

## Museum Guide

Robots guide visitors through exhibitions.

Capabilities:

- storytelling
- interactive displays
- multi-language interaction

---

## Security Patrol

Robots patrol facilities and monitor safety conditions.

Capabilities:

- video monitoring
- anomaly detection
- patrol routes

---

## Cleaning and Logistics

Robots perform operational tasks in facilities.

Capabilities:

- cleaning
- goods transportation
- warehouse operations
